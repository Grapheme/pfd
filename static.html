How it works?

An Introducing Broker (IB) is a person or a company which refers clients to PFD. As long as these clients trade, the IB receives rebate from PFD. It is a spread model, which is mutually profitable for both broker and IB.

How to become an IB? Open an account with PFD either online signup or paper mode application form like any other client using;

Account opened by an IB will be treated as a rebate-cum-trading account;
Download PFD’s Introducing Broker Agreement using;
PFD will discuss rebate structure with IB, accept an IB and align IB’s rebate-cum-trading account with the unique IB agreement;

How is IB’ rebate calculated? PFD operates on a transparent rebate structure. PFD offers base spreads as in PFDTrader (MT4) demo account to clients sourced by an IB and while IB’s clients enjoy base spreads, PFD will rebate 0.50 pips RT to IB. IB ineligible for rebates from scalping trades.

How and when will an IB get the rebate credited? PFD allows real-time crediting of eligible rebate to IB’s account as and when an IB’s client closes a trade.

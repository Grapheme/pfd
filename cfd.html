CFD

PFD offers below products as non-commission Contract For Differences (CFDs)/Over The Counter (OTC) solutions for its clients and under special trading terms. CFDs are financial derivatives that allow traders to take advantage of prices moving up or prices moving down on underlying financial instruments (such as a share) without the need to physically own such assets/instruments and are often used to speculate on those markets.
